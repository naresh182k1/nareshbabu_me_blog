## Overview

McKinsey's annual AI survey covers over 1,000 enterprises globally. This edition reveals how organizations are moving from AI experimentation to production-scale deployment.

## Key Findings

- Organizations with enterprise-wide AI strategies see 3x higher ROI
- MLOps maturity is the strongest predictor of AI success
- 65% of enterprises report significant revenue impact from AI
- Talent and data quality remain the top barriers to AI adoption
