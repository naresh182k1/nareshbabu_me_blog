## Overview

This report examines the critical data and analytics trends that technology leaders need to understand and act on in 2026. Gartner analysts identify the key patterns that will define successful data strategies.

## Key Findings

- AI-augmented analytics will be adopted by 75% of enterprises by end of 2026
- Data fabric architectures will reduce data management effort by 70%
- Graph analytics will grow 100% annually through 2026
- Active metadata management will be essential for data governance
